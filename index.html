Bio

I am a graduate student at Caltech within the department of Environmental Science and Engineering, and I have a B.A. in applied mathematics from UC Berkeley (2019). My research interests still very broad; I am fascinated by climate dynamics in general, especially on the interfaces between climate, mathematics, and computer science. In undergrad, I worked with Burkhard Militzer on planetary interiors and William Boos on monsoon depressions and ITCZ dynamics.
